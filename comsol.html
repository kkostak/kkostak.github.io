CFD

Fluid & Heat Transfer Models of Cerebrospinal Fluid

This was a project done during my studies of Thermal Fluid Systems. At the beginning of this project, I was very much ambitious of the capabilites of Comsol, and created a CAD model of the region between the brain and the skull using a 3D scan of a real brain. However, the nature of the brain being such a complex organic shaped proved to be a monumental challenge for simulation software to tackle, and thus we ended up creating a extremely simplified version to get a rough idea of the fluid movement and heat transfer that occurs within the skull.
