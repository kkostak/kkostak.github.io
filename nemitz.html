Soft-Bodied Robotics

Project Summary

Currently, I am working with PhD Student Yijia Wu to research applications of pellet printing, a new 3D printing technique that uses raw pellets as opposed to the convemtional filament strands. By 3D printing with pellets, we are able to create the same complex designs that a conventional 3D printer is able to, but with the advantage that we can create them with ultra-soft and flexible materials! My role in this research is to create and test demonstrations on the capabilites of the pellet printer, to show that designs created through pellet printed are just as good, if not better, than soft designs that are traditionally made through silicone molds. My current design is one of a hand where every joint is individually controllable using air pressure.
