Laser Drilling @ NCKU

GOALS: Design a COMSOL simulation that can simulate laser drilling on Aluminum Oxide and Aluminum Nitride & design an experiment to determine optimal laser drilling parameters

SKILLS: COMSOL Multiphysics, Experimental Design, Simulation Validation, MATLAB

I spent the summer of 2025 in Taiwan studying laser drilling manufacturing on semiconducor materials under Professor Yu-Lung Lo. This simulation uses a femtosecond laser at 50 kHz & 400 laser pulses at a peak power of 24 W.
